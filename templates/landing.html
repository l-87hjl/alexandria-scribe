Alexandria Scribe

A preservation‑first system for ingesting, exploring, and recombining ideas.

1. Disassemble (Intake)

Upload documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV) and break them into preservable fragments. Nothing is saved unless you explicitly ingest it.

Go to Disassembler

2. Explore (Archive)

Browse and search preserved fragments. Use similarity signals to explore without creating concepts, categories, or structure.

Go to Fragment Browser

3. Recombine (Export)

Assemble fragments into reports, outlines, or articles. Export results as files without altering the underlying archive.

Go to Recombulator
Stage 1 is frozen. Stage 2 emergence is design‑only.
