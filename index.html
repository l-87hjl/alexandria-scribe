Alexandria Scribe

A preservation‑first system for ingesting, exploring, and recombining ideas.

1. Disassemble (Intake)

Upload documents (PDFs, text, archives) and break them into preservable fragments. Review and download previews before committing anything to memory.

→ Go to Disassembler

2. Explore (Archive)

Browse and search preserved fragments. Adjust your view using similarity signals without creating concepts, categories, or structure.

→ Go to Fragment Browser

3. Recombine (Export)

Assemble fragments into reports, outlines, or articles. Export results as files without altering the underlying archive.

→ Go to Recombulator

Nothing is saved unless you explicitly choose to ingest or preserve it.
